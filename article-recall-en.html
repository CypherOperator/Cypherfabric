Windows Recall : Is Microsoft spying on you?

Published by Admin | Category : Privacy

Recently, a Microsoft feature has shaken the cybersecurity world: Windows Recall.

1. What is "Recall" ?

Imagine your PC taking a screenshot of everything you do, every few seconds. That's the principle behind Recall.

2. The Security Danger.

If a hacker gains access to your PC, they no longer need to search for your passwords; they simply need to look at Recall's visual history..
